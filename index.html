About Graphaxe

Welcome to the future of 2D game development. Graphaxe is an advanced, high-octane game engine powered by Haxe, designed for developers who refuse to compromise. With Graphaxe, you can craft visually stunning, highly optimized games that blow your audience away. Whether you're a solo creator or part of a battle-hardened team, Graphaxe arms you with the tools, power, and speed to turn your boldest visions into reality.

Features

  • ⚡ Blazing-fast rendering with hardware acceleration to keep the action smooth
  • 🛠️ Fully customizable workflows—adapt to any challenge with ease
  • 🎨 Cutting-edge graphical effects, including dynamic shaders and post-processing magic
  • 📜 Cross-platform support that dominates Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android, and HTML5
  • 🎮 Full console support for PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch—because why not?
  • 🚀 HTML5 deployment that's fast, efficient, and powerful—get your game out there
  • 💻 Engine IDE with a slick, user-friendly interface—because power shouldn’t come at the cost of usability
  • 📝 Built entirely in Haxe for maximum performance and type-safe development
  • 📜 Lua scripting language integration for crafting game logic with finesse
  • 🖥️ JavaScript scripting support for those who live on the bleeding edge
  • 🎛️ Multitexture support for the most complex rendering scenarios
  • 📦 Hyper-efficient batching—render more, do more, with less
  • 🖼️ Texture Atlas support to streamline asset management
  • 🔧 Custom shaders to push visual boundaries and break the mold
  • 🔁 Advanced scene management system—organize, control, conquer (Scenegraph)
  • 🖱️ Seamless input handling for keyboards, mice, touchscreens, and game controllers
  • 🎵 Immersive 2D and 3D audio engine to bring your game world to life
  • 💾 Robust asset management system—because your game’s success starts with organization
  • 🔗 Networking support built-in—multiplayer, online features, it’s all here
  • 📈 Powerful debugging and profiling tools—because perfection demands precision
  • 🔄 Hot-reload support—see your changes in real-time, no delays, no hassle
  • 🌐 Multilingual support to reach gamers worldwide
  • 🔐 Secure save system with cutting-edge encryption
  • 🚧 Modular architecture for easy customization and expansion—your engine, your rules
  • 📊 Integrated analytics and telemetry for real-time data and player insights
  • 📋 Extensive documentation backed by an active, passionate community
